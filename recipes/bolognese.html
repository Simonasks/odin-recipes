This is a bolognese recipe

In this recipe we'll show you how to make the best bolognese in the world.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Spaghetti
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to instructions on packaging
  2. Fry minced beef
  3. Blend everythin together in a big bowl
  4. Serve to friends and family with some mozzarella cheese
